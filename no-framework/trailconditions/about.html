Built for runners, powered by runners.

We started TrailConditions after too many runs turned into slips or reroutes. Now local stewards keep the board alive with actionable updates.

Established 2019
70 local stewards
Open data friendly

Our mission

Keep every runner informed. Our mission is to share current trail conditions, highlight safety concerns, and celebrate the routes that make each region unique.

How it works

Community reports are verified by stewards who know each trail system. We publish only the most recent data and flag items that need follow up.
